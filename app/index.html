About company

The creation and development of Absolut Group is inextricably linked with the spread of cellular communications in our country. Its history begins in 1998, it was at that time that its own retail chain of stores selling mobile phones NMT, GSM was created, and services for their connection were also provided. Today Absolut Group is a well-known wholesale electronics supplier with an impeccable reputation. The Absolut Group's field of activity also includes such areas as financial management, logistics, information support.
